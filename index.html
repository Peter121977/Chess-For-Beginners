Welcome to the Limerick Chess Club

Limerick Chess Club is the newest chess club in Ireland (founded in 2023). We are located in the United Arts Club at 3, Oconnell Stret. This central south city venue offers convenient access – see location page on this site for full details.

We are the only chess club in Limerick to have Chess boards permanently set up and ready for play as shown in the photo of our playing room which is dedicated to chess.

Our playing room also features a large wall mounted chess display board which is used for analysis sessions.
